💩 Simi sai?

Tired of building the next unicorn? Take a break from making the world a better place and join us at the first Super Silly Hackathon!!!

Come together with fellow developers, engineers and designers to build cool projects with absolutely no value whatsoever. Yes, the more boliao the better.

💡 Ideas

Too smart to think of something silly? Here are some ideas:

  • Eyeball Pong

  • Zippable Banana
    A banana peel that you can zip back up later.
  • Beer Selfie Stick
    A selfie stick that lets you pour beer in your mouth before you take a photo.

👐 Code of Conduct

Everyone is welcomed at the Super Silly Hackathon so join us! Don't shy.

Things you can/should do at the Super Silly Hackathon:

  • Make super silly things
  • Have fun, make friends

Things you cannot do at the Super Silly Hackathon:

  • Racist/sexist/discriminatory things
  • Harass or insult other participants

We will remove you from the event or alert the appropriate authorities if you endanger others or act inappropriately. Don't anyhow hor, or you confirm kena.

If someone is abusive or causes you to feel uncomfortable, speak to the organizers, or email us at hello@supersillyhackathon.sg

We pinky swear we will do right by you.
